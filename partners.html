How it works

Three steps to your first commission.

1
Apply and get approved.

Fill in the short application below. We review every application personally and get back to you within 2 business days. Once approved, you get your unique referral link and access to your partner dashboard.

2
Share Bazzcards with your network.

Introduce Bazzcards to professionals, businesses, HR teams, and organisations in your network. Use your referral link, share it on WhatsApp, LinkedIn, email — however you communicate. Every visit tracked back to you counts.

3
Earn on every confirmed sale.

When a lead you referred purchases — whether it's a single personal card or a corporate order of 100 — you earn commission. Tracked automatically in your dashboard. Paid out once confirmed.

Who should apply

Built for people who know people.

You don't need to be a salesperson. You need to know businesses, professionals, or organisations who could use a smarter way to connect.

Individual Networkers

Consultants, business coaches, event organisers, community leaders, content creators with a professional audience. If you move in circles where business cards come up — this is for you.

Agencies & Service Businesses

Marketing agencies, PR firms, HR consultancies, business registration services, printing companies. If you already serve businesses that would benefit from Bazzcards — add it to what you offer.

Corporate & Institutional Contacts

Anyone with access to companies, banks, hotels, hospitals, law firms, schools, or large organisations. A single corporate referral can be a significant order — and your commission reflects that.

Why it works

A product that sells itself.
A commission that rewards you.

The product does the talking.

When someone taps a Bazzcard for the first time, they want one. You don't have to convince people — you just have to introduce them. The demo is built into every card already in the market.

02
Corporate orders mean serious commission.

A 50-card corporate order at UGX 70,000 per card is a UGX 3,500,000 order. At 5% that's UGX 175,000 from one referral. At 10% that's UGX 350,000. One good corporate introduction goes a long way.

You always know where you stand. Every click, every referral, every confirmed order and every pending payout — visible in real time. No chasing us for updates.
