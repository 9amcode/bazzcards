How it works

Simple for employees.
Powerful for the people who manage them.

From setup to first tap in days — not weeks.

1

Every employee gets a card, ready from day one.

Each team member receives a personalised NFC card pre-loaded with their name, title, and company profile. No technical setup on their end. HR or Admin configures everything from one central dashboard before the cards even arrive.

2

One tap shares their complete professional profile.

Tap the card to any smartphone — the recipient sees the employee's full profile instantly. Contact details, social links, company information. They save it in seconds. No app, no friction, no fumbling with paper cards that might be in the wrong bag.

3

You stay in control, always.

Someone got promoted? Update their title remotely in seconds. Someone left the company? Deactivate their card immediately from the dashboard — their profile goes offline instantly, protecting your company data. No chasing people for old cards.

4

Every introduction becomes a captured lead.

When someone views an employee's card, they can save the contact and share theirs back with one tap. Leads land in your dashboard automatically. No manual entry, no lost business cards at the bottom of someone's bag, no missed follow-ups.
